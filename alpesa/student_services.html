Pre-Departure Orientation Session

- Selecting a program of study and location as well as cost considerations, let alone a career path, may be a source of anxiety for students preparing for undergraduate or graduate studies.
- AES provides one-on-one customized mentoring, on-line webinars, and on-site orientation visits to ease concerns of families and students to find answers to complex questions in the higher education sector such as selecting a major and career prospects upon graduation.
- Our experienced team guides students and their families in consideration of decisions related to the institution, location, campus culture, climate, safety, costs, accommodations, transportation, and other logistical details.
- Our advisors provide a comprehensive checklist for required documents which may include a valid passport, bank financial security letter, educational certificates, transcript evaluations, and test scores for eligibility.
- AES’s goal is to provide sufficient counseling ahead of decisions to avoid misinformation, loss of time, and personal funds.

- AES’s cross-cultural team guides students in the preparation of their resume to capture their education and experience.
- Our team provides counseling regarding the particulars of a personal statement of purpose usually required for admission.
- Some institutions require essays as part of the application. Our advisors will guide our students to prepare a compelling essay based on their future academic, research, and professional interests.
- As needed, on a case-by-case basis, AES offers a letter of recommendation on behalf of a student for admission, scholarship needs, and professional development training.

- The visa application can be an overwhelming process, particularly for first time entry students. AES provides a free checklist for reference as well as needed guidance.
- Our team will help ensure that the visa application is accurately completed for consulate processing and that visa interview protocols are adhered to for clearance.

- During counseling sessions, AES advisors provide students with a financial checklist to project realistic estimated costs for academic program, extracurricular activities, accommodations, insurance, transportation, and other related costs.
- AES provides guidance on financial assistance, scholarships, and loans available to students in their home country, as well as in the U.S. in pursuing their higher education dream

- AES’s advisors introduce students to campus culture and surrounding communities, encouraging them to engage in volunteer service activities as part of their professional development.
- Our team provides counseling for on-campus paid work-study, internships, practical training, graduate education, research, and future employment.

- AES assists with locating on-campus as well as off-campus accommodations.
- Our students will benefit from AES’s established residential network via one of the best real estate companies in the U.S., particularly for off-campus housing.

- AES assists students with their travel plans since for many students this is the first time to travel out of their home country.
- Our team is networked with many travel agencies and consultants who will help identify the best routes and costs for travel to the U.S.

Free International Sim
  - AES is always prepared to assist with any communication needs students may experience on route or upon arrival on campus.
  - AES provides free international SIM to our students to facilitate their communicating with our team, as well as their loved ones, until they are settled on campus.
Transcript Courier Services
  - AES helps students with transcript courier services, as needed.
Forex Assistance
  - AES assists students with foreign exchange currency transactions, as needed.
