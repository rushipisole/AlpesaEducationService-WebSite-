    Corporate Capabilities

    Alpesa Education Services, Inc., (AES) located in Pune, Maharashtra, India, was established in spring 2022 to nurture students to achieve their higher education aspirations while serving as a catalyst for campus international enrollment and partnership initiatives. Into the future, AES will lead an effort for partner campuses, membership organizations, and international agencies to collaborate with global employers to increase internships, practical training, and employment for our students and graduates.
       Global Education Services (GESI), a partner company located in Nashville, Tennessee USA, was also established in spring 2022, to complement and provide a rapid response platform for global programs and services.
       Within the year, GESI has signed contracts with Nine (9) campuses to include Austin Peay State University, East TN State University, Mercyhurst University, Middle TN State University, Milwaukee School of Engineering, Tennessee State University, Tennessee Technological University, Trevecca Nazarene University, and Westcliff University.
       In 2022, GESI chose to target India as its first country for outreach based on the new 2020 India National Education Policy that welcomes various forms of international education exchanges and linkages. AES & GESI will focus on Tier II and Tier III regions within the 36 states in India to provide fair access to deserving students seeking higher education abroad. In fall 2022, AES and GESI began assisting U.S. campuses with customized tours to India to recruit students, develop joint academic partnership linkages, and prospect to establish branch campuses abroad to expand access and outreach. In spring 2023, AES has registered 120 Indian students to be admitted to U.S. universities in fall 2023 and spring 2024. GESI will add a new country to its portfolio each year and expand recruitment and other partnership prospects into the future.
       GESI is a member to NAFSA-Association of International Educators, Tennessee Association of International Educators (TAIE), Study TN, Nashville Area Chamber of Commerce, and the United Nations Association of the USA. AES & GESI is in active communications with the federal Education USA State Department’s global network.
       GESI presented on the theme of Building Partnerships in India at the 2023 annual spring TAIE conference in Chattanooga, TN. GESI will be leading an effort in the state of TN for International Education Week in fall 2023.

    Why AES?

    ● Creates personalized pathways for international students to pursue post-secondary education in the U.S. with access to 4,000 public and private colleges and universities.
    ● Develops customized opportunities for U.S. campuses to build new partnerships with campuses abroad for academic, research, satellite campus, and other innovations.
    ● Develops ongoing collaborations with global employers to increase internships, practical training, and employment for graduates.
    ● Staff have first-hand knowledge to meet expectation of U.S. campuses as well as the preparation necessary for student success.
    ● Students receive a lifetime AES ID to pursue their education, to be employed, and to be active alumni to give back to society.
